What is a Card Memory Game?

  1. The objective is to turn over all of the pairs of cards in a few a moves as possible!
  2. One wrong guess minuses one from your score.
  3. A correct match pluses two to your score.
  4. To chose the card you want to reveal simply click on one of the star images. Then click on one more to see if you can match them up!
  5. You will get glimpse of where the cards are at the start of the game and with each card tun - so try to remember where they all are!
  6. When you get all the pairs you win and you can go on to a harder level or try and beat your last score.
  7. Remember to have fun and enjoy yourself with this relaxing memory game.
